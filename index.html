Marcus Aurelius

121 - 180 A.D.

  • 26 April 121 - Born in Rome, Italy as Marcus Annius Verus.
  • After his father's passing, raised by paternal grandfather, Marcus Annius Verus (II).
  • Showed interest in wrestling and boxing. Joined the Salii.
  • Homeschooled and introduced to philosophy by Diognetus. Marcus takes up the role of philosopher.
